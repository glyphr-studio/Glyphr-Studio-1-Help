Edit History Panel

(Glyph Edit, Components, Ligatures, and Kerning Pages)

This panel shows a list of edits that are available to undo. There is a description of the action, along with a timestamp, and these actions are grouped by glyph name. Pressing the 'Undo' button, or Ctrl+z will reverse edits that are listed here.

Glyph Edit, Components, and Ligatures pages all have separate undo lists. The Edit History Panel will only show edits that have taken place on that particular page, not across Glyphr Studio as a whole.

Chooser Panel

(Glyph Edit, Components, Ligatures, and Import SVG Pages)

The Chooser Panel displays characters that are available to be selected. On the Glyph Edit, Components, or Ligatures pages, selecting a glyph from the Chooser Panel will load the Edit Canvas with whatever glyph you selected. On the Import SVG page, selecting a glyph from the Chooser Panel determines where your imported SVG shape outlines will be placed.
